What do you actually get from a referral when you buy a new Tesla in 2026? Here's the overview - and why you should always check the current figure.

Model 3 and Model Y: free Supercharging

For the two most popular models, the benefit is typically free Supercharging for a set number of kilometres. That's a concrete saving on your first many trips and makes the switch to an EV cheaper from the start. To understand charging better, watch for our upcoming guides on charging and Supercharging.

Model S and Model X: credit or discount

For the more expensive models, the benefit has historically been a cash discount or an account credit usable for things like Supercharging, accessories or software. Here too, Tesla sets the amount.

Does it apply to you as the referrer too?

Yes - the referral program usually rewards both parties: the new buyer and the existing owner who shares their code. That's the whole idea behind the program, and it's why I share my code here.

How long do the benefits last?

Free Supercharging credits normally have a validity period - often cited as 12 months from when they're granted. Once they're on your account, you can track usage in the Tesla app.

Benefits can change

History shows Tesla can both increase and reduce benefits, and the program can look different from market to market. Some analysts expect free Supercharging may eventually be supplemented or replaced by, for example, free Full Self-Driving trials as the features roll out in Europe.
