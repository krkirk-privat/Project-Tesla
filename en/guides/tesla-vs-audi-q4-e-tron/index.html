Tesla vs Audi Q4 e-tron

The Tesla Model Y and Audi Q4 e-tron both target the premium buyer, but with different takes on what "premium" means. Here are the differences.

Last updated: June 2026

Comparing the Tesla Model Y with the Audi Q4 e-tron pits Tesla's tech and efficiency focus against Audi's classic premium feel and finish. The Q4 e-tron shares its platform with the VW ID.4 but raises the level on materials and finish. Specific figures change, always confirm current specs with the manufacturers.

Where the Model Y typically shines

  • Supercharger with automatic payment is hard to beat on simplicity.
  • Software & OTA: the car keeps improving.
  • The Model Y often delivers longer real-world range and a large, flexible boot.
  • Referral benefit: free Supercharging or a discount, see current benefits.

Where the Audi Q4 e-tron typically shines

  • Materials and finish consistently hit a high level.
  • Comfort: a hushed, pleasant driving experience.
  • Brand and dealer network: a well-established luxury brand with a service network.

Which should you choose?

Choose the Model Y if charging network, software, efficiency and space weigh heaviest, and you want the referral benefit on top. Choose the Audi Q4 e-tron if build quality, comfort and a classic premium cabin matter most. Test-drive both. See also Model Y vs VW ID.4 and Tesla vs Polestar 2.
