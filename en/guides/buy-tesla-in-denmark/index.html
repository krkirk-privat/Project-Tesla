How to buy a Tesla in Denmark

Buying a Tesla works differently from a traditional dealer. Here's the process from start to delivery - and where the referral code fits in.

Last verified: 24 April 2026

Tesla sells directly to customers without a classic dealer network. That means the whole purchase can in practice be done online - but there are still stores and delivery centres in Denmark where you can see and collect the cars. Here are the steps.

1. Choose model and variant

Start by finding the right model. Do you want the affordable sedan, the family SUV or the flagship? Read our model guides for the Model 3 and Model Y - and consider a comparison of the two if you're unsure.

2. Use the referral link BEFORE you order

This is the most important step for getting the benefit: open the referral link before you place the order. That links the referral to your order, and the benefit (e.g. free Supercharging) is registered automatically. See the full process in the guide how to use a Tesla referral code.

3. Configure the car

On tesla.com you choose paint, wheels, interior and any options. The price updates as you go, so you can see the total. You can also choose an inventory car if you want faster delivery.

4. Pay the deposit and confirm

The order is confirmed with a deposit. You then get an estimated delivery time. Note that deposit and cancellation terms can vary - read the conditions when ordering.

5. Financing or leasing

You can pay cash, finance or lease. If you're weighing leasing versus buying, read our guide on the topic when it's ready - it comes down to how you want to own the car and your financial situation.

6. Delivery and registration

Tesla handles registration as part of the delivery process, and you typically collect the car at a delivery centre or have it delivered. At handover, it's a good idea to inspect the car thoroughly. Bring ID and the documents Tesla asks for.

The key things to remember

  • Use the referral link before you confirm the order.
  • Check the current referral benefit and price on tesla.com.
  • Inspect the car thoroughly at delivery.

Taxes and economics

The price of an EV in Denmark is affected by registration tax and the rules in force at the time of purchase. The rules can change, so always check the current rates and the final price with Tesla. We publish more on Danish EV economics in our guides.
