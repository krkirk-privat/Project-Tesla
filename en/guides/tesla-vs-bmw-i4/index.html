Tesla vs BMW i4

The Tesla Model 3 and BMW i4 are both premium electric sedans - but with classic BMW DNA against Tesla's tech approach. Here are the differences.

Last verified: 12 March 2026

If the choice is between a Tesla Model 3 and a BMW i4, you're choosing between two schools of thought. BMW offers a classic, polished driving experience and a traditional premium cabin; Tesla bets on software, efficiency and its own charging network. Specific figures for range and price change - always confirm current specs with the manufacturers.

Where the Model 3 typically shines

  • Charging network: Supercharger with automatic payment is hard to beat on simplicity.
  • Software & OTA: the car keeps improving.
  • Efficiency: often longer real-world range per kWh and typically a lower price.
  • Referral benefit: free Supercharging or a discount - see current benefits.

Where the BMW i4 typically shines

  • Driving experience: classic BMW dynamics and finish that many enthusiasts value.
  • Interior: traditional cabin with premium materials and physical controls.
  • Brand feel: a well-established luxury brand with a dealer network.

Which should you choose?

Choose the Model 3 if software, efficiency, charging network and price weigh heaviest - and you want the referral benefit on top. Choose the BMW i4 if a classic driving experience and a traditional premium cabin matter most. Test-drive both. See also Tesla vs Polestar 2 and Model 3 vs Model Y.
