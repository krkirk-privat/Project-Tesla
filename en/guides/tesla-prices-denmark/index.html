Tesla prices in Denmark 2026

How much does a Tesla cost in Denmark? Here's an indicative overview of the starting prices - and an explanation of what pushes the price up and down.

Last verified: 15 June 2026

Important about the prices

The prices below are indicative starting prices based on publicly available Danish sources in 2026 and may have changed by the time you read this. Tesla adjusts prices regularly, and the final price depends on variant, options and taxes. Always confirm the current price on tesla.com.

The Model 3 Standard has been advertised as Tesla's most affordable model in Denmark to date, with a starting price around DKK 270,000. The Model Y starts higher, as it's a larger SUV. Top variants and options raise the price considerably - a well-equipped Model 3, for example, can reach well above the starting price.

What affects the price?

  • Model and variant - Standard, Long Range or Performance.
  • Paint, wheels and interior - options are added on top.
  • Software - e.g. the Full Self-Driving package.
  • Taxes - registration tax and the current Danish rules.
  • Campaigns and referral - can lower the effective price.
