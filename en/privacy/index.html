Privacy

Privacy & cookie policy

The short version: the site only measures anonymous stats, and only if you say yes. Below you can read what is collected, why, and how to change your choice at any time.

Last updated: June 2026

Who is behind the site

tesla-rabatkode.dk is a private site I run as a Tesla owner. You can read more about me and the referral programme on the about page. You control your consent to analytics right here on the site, and further down you can read about your rights and how to withdraw it.

What I measure

I use Google Analytics 4 to see which guides get used, so I can improve them. This only happens once you've given consent. The statistics are anonymous and aggregated: which pages are visited, roughly which country or region the visit comes from, the type of device and browser, and which source you came from (such as a search). I cannot see who you are, and I don't collect your name, email or other details that directly identify you.

Cookies and consent

The first time you visit, I ask whether analytics may be switched on. If you click Allow, Google Analytics sets cookies that help count visits. If you click Decline, analytics stays off and no analytics cookies are set. Your choice is stored locally in your browser so you aren't asked every time. You can change your choice at any time here:

Beyond analytics, the site uses only the bare essentials (such as remembering your cookie choice). There are no third-party advertising or tracking cookies for marketing.

Legal basis

The processing is based on your consent, under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Article 6(1)(a). You can withdraw your consent at any time, just as easily as you gave it, using the button above.

Google as data processor

Google Analytics is provided by Google. When analytics is switched on, Google processes data on my behalf, and data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EU/EEA. You can read how Google handles data in Google's privacy policy, and you can install Google's opt-out add-on for your browser if you want to avoid Google Analytics on all websites.

How long data is kept

The aggregated statistics are kept by Google for a limited period under Google's default retention settings. You can always withdraw your consent so that nothing more is collected.

Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification and erasure of your data, to object to the processing, and to withdraw your consent. Because the statistics are anonymous and aggregated, I usually cannot link them to a specific person. If you believe your data is being handled incorrectly, you can complain to the Danish Data Protection Agency.

Referral link

If you use my referral code when you buy a Tesla, I may receive a benefit from Tesla. It doesn't affect your data and doesn't require cookies on this page. There's more about it on the about page.

Changes

I may update this policy if the site or the rules change. The date of the latest update is shown at the top.
